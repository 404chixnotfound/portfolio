About

Renovating Technology.

LocationAbuja, Nigeria
FocusGame / App Development
StatusOpen to work

I’m a software developer obsessed with the idea that code can be both a precision tool and a canvas. I aim to build apps and games that feel alive, and tools that quietly reshape how we interact with technology.

My playground spans from low-level game loops and fluid UI architectures to cloud‑native backends and blockchain tech. But what really drives me is renovation: taking familiar software ideas apart, cleaning the gears, and putting them back together in ways nobody’s seen yet.

I’m drawn to the indie spirit, small teams, big imaginations, and the belief that a single person with a strong vision can make something unforgettable. Whether I watch someone crafts a stylized indie game, a productivity app that feels like a breath of fresh air, or an experimental dev tool, I aim for the same thing: work that quietly raises the bar.
